Just for today I will take care of my body. I will exercise it, care for it, nourish it, not abuse it or neglect it. Just for today I will try to strengthen my mind. I will learn something useful. I will not be a mental loafer. I will read something that requires effort, thought, and concentration. Just for today I will exercise my soul in three ways; I will do somebody a good turn and not get found out. I will do at least 2 things I don’t want to do, as William James suggests, just for exercise(“Everybody should do at least two things each day that he hates to do, just for practice.”)
